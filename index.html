Quick Notes

Bond Formation & Electronegativity: A bond is formed between two elements. The score you get depends on the **difference in electronegativity** between the elements. A larger difference in electronegativity creates a stronger bond, and a larger score!

Atomic Radius: This is the size of the atom. In a duel, a smaller atomic radius (a lower value) is better!

Ionization Energy: This is the energy required to remove an electron from an atom. In a duel, a higher ionization energy (a higher value) is better!
